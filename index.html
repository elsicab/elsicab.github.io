About Me

Hello there! My name is Elsa Caballero and I am a software developer and a forever student.

When I was younger I always thought my dream was to become a pediatrician and thus I followed that path into my college years. But at a certain point, I realized that dream had fluttered away, and I began looking for a new spark. When I first tried coding, there was no spark but a full-blown flame.

Today, I am a software engineer with experience in multiple development languages and tools such as Java, JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby, and Rails. I am constantly learning, growing, and developing.

Little Heist

-Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Canvas API-

Little Heist is a mini-game which attempts to mimic the style of a coin collection and obstacle avoidance game. The objective is to have the player reach the next level by collecting coins and avoiding the guards. The game ends when the player is caught by the guard. The game is won when the player collects 30 coins.

WiredIn is a functional LinkedIn clone in which an user can create an account and profile and connect with other members. The user has the ability to create, edit and delete posts as well as edit all basic profile information. They may also view other memeber's profiles.

Come What May

-MongoDB, Express, React/Redux, Node, Heroku-

Come What May is a disaster preparation app fully customizable to the needs of any household. When preparation is key, Come What May allows users to create a step-by-step plan to practice with the family. Each plan is carefully orchestrated by the account owner and tested for time efficiency using the built-in drills and drill history collection.
